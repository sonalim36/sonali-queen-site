QUEEN

The Rhapsody Tour

The Rhapsody Tour is an ongoing worldwide concert tour by Queen + Adam Lambert, the collaboration between British rock band Queen and American singer Adam Lambert. The tour was announced following the success of the biopic film Bohemian Rhapsody.
